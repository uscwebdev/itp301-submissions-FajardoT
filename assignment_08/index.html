Assignment 08: Final Project Proposal

Proposal

Topic: My website would be similar to other music streaming platforms where I would offer playlists and reccomendations for listeners trying to find a new artists to follow.

Audience: My audience would primarily target those interested in Spanish Trap music as those are the artists I am currently most familar with. Additionally, they would be people who don't necessarily know the artists' catalogues and instead are trying to see where to start.

Design and Style: I want my color scheme to be monochromatic with little deviation from the Golden Rod Color. The primary color would be Golden Rod with text being white and and thinking about using black for when buttons are being hovered over/pressed.

List of Websites I will like to take inspiration from...

  • Spotify
  • Apple Music
  • Youtube Music

Sections: I am currently thinking of having three sections for the webpage. They would be the main page which would act like a gallery of artists that the visitor can hover and click over for more info about them and give them the option to go to another one of the site's pages.

The second page technically is custom for each featured artist but would provide the same funciton. It would list their biggest hits as well as their past works. In addition to this, it would include various clips/videos that would be related to their creative process such as interviews/podcasts that would help offer insight as to who they are as a person/persona.

The final section would be a library of curated playlists that are organized based on "moods" that the listener might be looking for. They would also be seperated by the artist instead of a few lists of the overall genre.

Extras: Currently the extras would involve some music videos from the artists for a select number of songs. Additionally, I would want to have the background change while the user scrolls down to either a video or image of the partiuclar time the song/album they're looking at released.
